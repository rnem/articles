Amazon Web Services (AWS) has announced the preview of AWS Interconnect, multicloud, a new managed capability providing simple, resilient, high-speed private connections between Amazon VPCs and other cloud providers (starting with Google Cloud, with Azure announced for 2026).

This moves away from "do-it-yourself" multicloud networking to provide a native, standardized way for clouds to connect.

In just a few steps in the AWS Management Console or via CLI, you can: 
1. Choose the target cloud provider 
2. Select the destination region 
3. Pick the required bandwidth

AWS and the other CSP handle provisioning, capacity, resiliency, and support, giving you a single attachment representing your network capacity to that provider.
